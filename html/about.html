About Us


Welcome to the best flower shop chain in the country!


In 1967, in a small colony in the north of the country, the first store was established by Israel Hasbani. So married to Rina and father to Yoram and Sharona. The store was established with a lot of love and respect for the plant and its beauty. Since then our flower shops have supplied hundreds of thousands of flowers and plants to all the inhabitants of the country, who came from all over its ends. Grandpa Israel's legacy continues to this day by his grandchildren Danny and Sean. Come join our thriving and flourishing family!
